Delete Your Receipty NZ Account

This page explains how to delete your Receipty NZ account and what happens to your data when you do.

How to Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your account directly from the app:

  1. Open the Receipty NZ app on your device.
  2. Sign in if you are not already signed in.
  3. Tap the Settings tab at the bottom of the screen.
  4. Scroll down to the Account section.
  5. Tap Delete Account.
  6. Confirm the deletion in the dialog that appears.
Account deletion is immediate and permanent. Once you confirm, your account and all associated data will be deleted from our servers and cannot be recovered.

What Data is Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your account profile (email, name, profile picture)
  • All receipt records you have created
  • All line items extracted from your receipts
  • All notes, categories, and use-type tags you have added
  • App preferences and settings linked to your account

What Data is Retained

In rare cases, we may retain limited information for legal, security, or fraud-prevention purposes, such as records required by tax authorities or payment-related logs. Any retained data is anonymised where possible and kept only for as long as legally required.

Subscriptions

If you have an active Pro subscription, deleting your account does not automatically cancel it. To cancel your subscription, please visit your Google Play Store subscription settings before deleting your account.
